As a participant or trainee you will have the option to select a Training programme of your interest as core development track during the festival and beyond. Each area is tailored to match interests, career paths, and development goals, ensuring practical and relevant learning outcomes.

Model United Nations (MUN) and diplomacy

Delivered under the umbrella of the International Youth Diplomacy Conference (IYDC), this track sharpens negotiation, policy analysis, and global engagement skills. It also aligns with the principles of the Model United Nations, a program established by the United Nations to train young people in diplomacy and prepare them for potential roles within the UN.

Register now
Scope
Modeling the United Nations General Assembly committees and some agencies.
Mode
In-person and virtual sessions (discussions, deliberations, negotiations, speeches, position papers and resolutions).
Duration
One month pre-MUN training and two (2) days in-person training.

Governance and Leadership Training Programme

This program prepares young people to take on leadership roles in government, community, and organizational settings. It also equips current officials in these fields with the skills and knowledge needed to enhance their leadership impact.

Register now
Scope
Fellowship Programme.
Mode
In-person and virtual training (Practical Teamwork Activities, Soft Skills Training, Leadership Exercises, Brainstorming, Pitches, Coaching and Mentorship, Industry Tour etc).
Duration
Two (2) Days in person and One (1) year coaching.

Entrepreneurship Training Programme

This program provides practical training in business development, startup strategies, and financial literacy. Given that entrepreneurship is a key solution to Africa's unemployment challenges, participants will receive comprehensive entrepreneurial training to develop essential skills in business management, financial planning, fundraising, and more.

Register now
Scope
Business Development Programme.
Mode
In-person and virtual training (Practical Teamwork Activities, Soft Skills Training, Leadership Exercises, Brainstorming, Pitches, Coaching and Mentorship, Industry Tour etc).
Duration
Two (2) Days in person and One (1) year virtual training and follow-up sessions.

Information and Technology Skills Enhancement (HO2WO)

In partnership with the Home to World Office (HO2WO) initiative by Bruce Boye Business Consulting (BBBC), you will receive IT skills enhancement as young IT professional for specialized job opportunities in Europe and Africa. Through this initiative, IT-skilled African youth particularly from Ghana will be empowered to work on European projects remotely. Our goal is to boost global competitiveness and open new career pathways for these talented individuals.

Register now
Scope
IT skills enhancement, Internship and job recruitment.
Mode
In-person and virtual training
Duration
Two (2) days and contract to contract periods.

Vocational & Technical Skills Enhancement Programme

In collaboration with industry experts, this programme is designed to equip young people with practical, hands-on skills in vocational and technical fields. Participants will gain real-world experience to help them thrive in today's changing job market. Ghanaian attendees will also be linked to a specialized program through Ghana's TVET and Youth Employment Agency, offering continued training and potential referrals to similar programs in other African countries.

Register now
Scope
Skills enhancement.
Mode
In-person Training.
Duration
Two (2) days during the festival and six (6) months of project work.

Creative Arts Talents Enhancement Programme

It is a talent enhancement programme for young Africans in the following industries: visual arts, dance, theater, poetry, and literature. There will be workshops including, marketing, business development and others. Also creatives at the festival will have the opportunity to showcase their talents.

Register now
Scope
Talent enhancement, Exhibitions and Performance.
Mode
In-person and virtual Training.
Duration
Three (3) days and One (1) year coaching.

Women's Wealth Building Training Programme

This training programme is designed to equip women with essential knowledge and practical skills in personal finance, savings, budgeting, investment, and access to microfinance. It aims to foster long-term economic independence and informed decision-making in both personal and business contexts.

Soft skills Training.
Mode
Soft skills Training.

Youth in Agribusiness Programme

Participants will explore how to transform traditional farming into profitable agribusiness ventures.
