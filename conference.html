The Africa Youth Festival promises to be a vibrant and enriching experience, combining knowledge-sharing, training, and cultural celebration to empower the next generation of African changemakers.

Conference, plenaries and seminars

The core of the festival will be an engaging conference featuring powerful opening and closing plenaries that set the tone and celebrate the outcomes of the entire gathering.

Throughout the festival, a series of interactive seminars will explore critical themes relevant to African youth today.

Personal and professional development
Equipping young people with the mindset and tools to grow in confidence, career, and character.
Social impact and civic engagement
Inspiring youth to be active citizens and lead change in their communities.
Health and well-being
Focusing on mental health, fitness, and access to quality care for a thriving generation.
Innovation, business and technology
Spotlighting opportunities in entrepreneurship, digital transformation, and emerging technologies.
Music, games and legacy-making moments
